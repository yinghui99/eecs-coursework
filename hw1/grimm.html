Brothers Grimm

Jacob Grimm (1785 - 1863) and Wilhem Grimm (1786 - 1859) are the famous German brothers who collaborated as authors, philologists, cultural researchers, and lexicographers to create the best-known folktales of the 19th century. They were among the first and best-known collectors of folk tales, and popularized traditional oral tales. (Wikipedia)
